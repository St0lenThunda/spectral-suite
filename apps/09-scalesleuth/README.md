1. **Start Detection**: Click "Start Scale Detection" to initialize the audio engine.
2. **Build a Set**: Play 3 or more distinct notes on your instrument. They will appear in the "Detected Set" area.
3. **Identify Scales**: Review the "Scale Suggestions" list. The percentage shows how well your notes fit each scale.
4. **Visualize**: Click a scale name to highlight its "safe" notes on the virtual fretboard.
5. **Reset**: Use "Reset Detective" to clear your note buffer and start a new discovery.
